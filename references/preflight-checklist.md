# Final preflight checklist

Run this checklist before final LaTeX packaging.
Report pass, partial pass, or fail for each category.

## 1. Coherence

Check:
- the title matches the actual argument
- the research question is explicit
- the article thesis is explicit
- each main section advances the thesis
- the discussion answers the question raised in the introduction
- the conclusion does not introduce new unsupported claims
- English and French versions follow the same argumentative structure

## 2. Originality

Check:
- the paper clearly states what is original about the extension
- historical description is not falsely presented as originality
- practical coaching innovation is distinguished from academic novelty
- the archive contribution is explicit
- the article does more than summarize the 2016 report
- the originality claim is proportionate to the actual evidence

## 3. Evidentiary balance

Check:
- strong claims rely on strong evidence
- speculative claims are signaled as interpretive or provisional
- archive sources and scholarly framing are balanced appropriately
- missing years or partial corpus coverage are acknowledged
- the freestyle contribution is neither ignored nor overstated
- expert-lens conclusions are integrated without pretending they are empirical findings themselves

## 4. Citation compliance

Check:
- the chosen style profile is recorded
- the citation policy is followed consistently
- the anchor report is cited where needed
- archive documents are cited as primary material
- theoretical framing claims are referenced adequately
- no placeholder citations remain

## 5. Section budget compliance

Check:
- section lengths remain close to the word-budget plan
- no section is underdeveloped relative to its role
- the introduction and discussion are not disproportionately short
- the article remains near the intended 20-page scale

## 6. Language and register

Check:
- English article is fully in English
- French article is fully in French
- orchestration notes are not left inside the article body
- academic register is consistent
- terminology is stable within each language

## 7. Packaging readiness

Check:
- `.tex` files are internally consistent
- bibliography file exists
- figure and table references resolve or are removed
- unresolved compile risks are listed clearly if present

## Final reporting template

Return a short final preflight note in this structure:
- coherence: pass | partial pass | fail
- originality: pass | partial pass | fail
- evidentiary balance: pass | partial pass | fail
- citation compliance: pass | partial pass | fail
- section budgets: pass | partial pass | fail
- packaging readiness: pass | partial pass | fail
- unresolved issues: [short list]
